First Aid for Choking

If someone is choking, follow these steps:

  1. Encourage them to cough.
  2. If unable to cough, perform 5 back blows.
  3. If needed, perform abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver).
  4. Call emergency services if the blockage is not cleared.

First Aid for Cuts

Follow these steps for minor cuts:

  1. Wash your hands thoroughly before treating the cut.
  2. Rinse the cut under clean, running water to remove dirt and debris.
  3. Gently clean around the cut with soap.
  4. Apply an antiseptic or antibiotic ointment.
  5. Cover the cut with a sterile bandage or gauze.
  6. Monitor for signs of infection, such as redness or swelling.
